# EC2 Pricing Models

### 1. On-Demand
- Pay per second/hour
- Best for short-term workloads
- No commitment

### 2. Reserved Instances (RI)
- 1 or 3-year commitment
- Cheap for predictable workloads

### 3. Savings Plans
- Flexible alternative to RI
- Commit to $ spend/hour

### 4. Spot Instances
- 90% cheaper
- Can be interrupted anytime

### 5. Dedicated Hosts
- Compliance / licensing needs
